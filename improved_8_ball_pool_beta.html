HOW TO
PLAY

Quick tutorial

1. Aim: Move your mouse around the white ball. The normal guide only gives a simple first-contact guide, so players still need skill.
2. Power: Hold click to charge. Release to shoot. Shot strength affects guide length and sound.
3. Spin: Use the hit-point panel in the bottom-left. Top spin follows through. Bottom spin pulls back. Side spin bends the cue ball after contact.
4. Fouls: You must hit the correct ball first. After contact, a ball must pot or hit a cushion. Fouls give ball in hand.
5. Admin: Press F2 to unlock hidden tools. Admin can enable full trajectory mode, which shows every moving ball path.
6. Polish: There are combo messages, match scores, adjustable difficulty, screen shake, shot timer and visual effects.
